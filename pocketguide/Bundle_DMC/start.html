A Peak Into D.M.C.’s Harlem

Check out the main spots in this shortcut and see how the Run-D.M.C. story began. Find Rice High School where Darryl started to write his first rhymes in the library. Pass by the place where Celebrity Club stood, a legendary spot for hip hop in the 80’s and visit the great Apollo Theater where Run-D.M.C. had its first live show in 1986. If you have limited time, take this 45 minutes walk and let D show you around.

The Full Experience

Come and explore the legendary places in Harlem for D.M.C. and the hip hop culture. The tour starts at Harlem Hospital and we’ll take you through all the iconic spots from Harlem World to Celebrity Club. Visit the Church of Intercession in Harlem which is rivalling the cathedrals of Europe and play basketball in Rucker Park, once the center of the hip-hop culture. Get an insight to the Run D.M.C. experience and learn background stories from one of its founding members, Darryl McDaniels.
